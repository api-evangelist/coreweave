CoreWeave has introduced unified agentic AI capabilities that enable organizations to deploy AI agents to production immediately and have them improve autonomously through real-world usage. The platform integrates serverless reinforcement learning, production-scale inference, observability tools, and autonomous improvement mechanisms into what the company calls the "superintelligence loop." This approach eliminates the traditional cycle of offline training and testing, allowing agents to learn from live production data and continuously enhance their reliability over time.
