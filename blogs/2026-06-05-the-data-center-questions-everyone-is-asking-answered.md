CoreWeave debunks seven common misconceptions about modern data centers, explaining that they do not raise electricity rates, actually support grid reliability through infrastructure upgrades, and operate quietly with acoustic controls. Data centers use minimal water through closed-loop cooling systems and generate substantial tax revenue while creating lasting jobs beyond construction. The post explains how data centers provide widespread economic benefits through infrastructure development that attracts additional business investment to communities.
